A Girl Named Izzy

There was a young girl, let's call her Dizzy.

One day she woke up late, and her hair was all frizzy,

But she said, "Let's fly!"

Because time was passing by.

She didn't think twice about living her life.

And she didn't seem to care about her family strife.

All she knew was here and now,

If only I could be her, someway, somehow.

Instead, I wallow in sorrow,

Because it's happiness I cannot borrow.

So I keep to myself and watch the sunset,

Because tomorrow's a new day, and I haven't given up, yet.

-Izzy
